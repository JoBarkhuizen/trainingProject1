Chicken Wraps

Serving: 2

  • 4 tbs Cross and Blackwell Mayonnaise
  • 0.5 tsp course black pepper
  • 1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon
  • Iceberg Lettuce - cut into small pieces
  • Tomato cut into small pieces
  • 2 Anat Wraps
  • 4 Pieces fried Crumbed Chicken Strips

Method:

Mix the Mayonnaise, black pepper and lemon together and put in fridge for about 1 hour. Take one Anat wrap and put some lettuce and tomato in the center. Put two of the Strips on the lettuce and drizzle with Mayonnaise. Fold and enjoy.

Savoury muffins with Feta cheese

Serving: about 24

  • 3⁄4 C Cream Cheese or Smooth Cottage Cheese
  • 5 Large eggs (room temperature) - beaten
  • 60 ml Coconut milk
  • 120 g Almond meal
  • 1⁄4 C (minus) 1Tbsp Flax Meal
  • 1 Tbsp Psyllium husk powder
  • 1 Tbsp Baking powder
  • 1⁄4 Tsp Salt
  • 1⁄2 C Crumbed Feta
  • 1⁄2 C Onion, finely chopped
  • 3 Tbsp Peppadew/Red pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 Tsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Coconut/olive oil

Method:

Preheat oven to 190Â°C. Coat a large muffin pan with cooking spray

Put oil, onion, peppadew, garlic in skillet and cook until soft, remove from heat and let cool

Whisk together cottage cheese, eggs and coconut milk. Stir dry ingredients in and whisk together until smooth.

Stir in onion mix and feta

Pour into muffin pan and bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown

Remove from oven and let cool for couple of minutes before removing from muffin pan. Let cool completely on wire rack
